ICJ Genocide Case (2024)

In December 2023, South Africa filed a case against Israel at the International Court of Justice (ICJ), accusing Israel of committing genocide against Palestinians in Gaza. The case is based on Israel's actions during its military campaign in Gaza following the October 7, 2023, attack by Hamas.
