Protective Edge Operation (2014)

Operation Protective Edge was a military operation launched by Israel in the Gaza Strip in July 2014. The operation lasted for 51 days and resulted in the deaths of more than 2,200 Palestinians, including a large number of civilians, and the destruction of thousands of homes and other infrastructure. UN schools were also bombed during the operation.
