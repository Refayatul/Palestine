Gaza Blockade Begins (2005)

The Gaza Blockade is an ongoing land, air, and sea blockade of the Gaza Strip imposed by Israel and Egypt in 2005, after Hamas won the Palestinian elections. The blockade has severely restricted the movement of people and goods in and out of Gaza, leading to a humanitarian crisis and widespread poverty.
