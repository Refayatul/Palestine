Palestinian Resistance
Through the Years

From stones to social media -
76 years of steadfast resistance against colonialism

Forms of Palestinian Resistance

Armed Resistance

1965: Founding of PLO

Palestine Liberation Organization established as united resistance front

  • Unified various factions under one organization
  • Recognized as sole legitimate representative of Palestinians
  • Launched operations against Israeli occupation

1987 & 2000: Intifadas

Popular uprisings combining armed and civil resistance

  • First Intifada (1987-1993): Stone-throwing youth vs. occupation
  • Second Intifada (2000-2005): Response to occupation brutality
  • 3,000+ Palestinians killed during uprisings

Civil Resistance

2018: Great March of Return

Peaceful protests at Gaza border fence

  • Weekly protests demanding right of return
  • 223 protesters killed by Israeli snipers
  • Included medics, journalists, disabled protesters
  • UN condemned Israel's use of live fire

Sheikh Jarrah Protests

Resistance to ethnic cleansing in Jerusalem

  • 2021 protests against forced expulsions
  • United Palestinians across all territories
  • Global solidarity movement #SaveSheikhJarrah
  • Led to temporary halt of evictions

Economic Resistance

BDS Movement

Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions campaign

  • Launched in 2005 by 170+ Palestinian organizations
  • Modeled after South African anti-apartheid movement
  • Key demands: End occupation, equal rights, right of return
  • Successes: Ben & Jerry's, Veolia, UN Human Rights blacklist

Agricultural Resistance

Sumoud ("steadfastness") through farming

  • Planting olive trees as symbols of rootedness
  • Resisting confiscation through cultivation
  • Tarabin Bedouins refusing forced displacement

Cultural Resistance

Art & Literature

Preserving identity through creative expression

  • Naji al-Ali's Handala cartoon symbol
  • Mahmoud Darwish's poetry of exile
  • Murals in refugee camps and on Israel's separation wall

Symbols of Resistance

Cultural icons asserting Palestinian identity

  • Keffiyeh headscarf as national symbol
  • Watermelon imagery when Palestinian flag was banned
  • Keys symbolizing right of return

Digital Resistance

Social Media Activism

Bypassing media blackouts to document occupation

  • #FreePalestine - Most trending hashtag in 2021
  • Palestinian journalists documenting war crimes
  • Bypassing censorship through alternative platforms

Alternative Media

Challenging biased mainstream narratives

  • Electronic Intifada, Mondoweiss, Middle East Eye
  • Palestinian podcasters and YouTubers
  • Graphic novels like "Palestine" by Joe Sacco
